Hello everyone!

My name is Jessi Cantellan, I am 27 years old and I am currently attending California State University Dominguez Hills as a Business Administration major with a concentration in Advanced Information Systems.

The Journey

Yes I am indeed 27 years old and on my 3rd year of college. When I was in high school I was too afraid to ask for help or any questions. This left me vulnerable and without the right resources to finish high school. My counselor placed me in the wrong classes for years, and when I found out I would not graduate it was too late. From then on I had no encouragement or motivation to continue my education and spent the next 6 years without a diploma. Until finally I decided to try once again, a journey that I thought would last me 2 to 3 years only took half a year.

Where am I now?!

After working a decent job that unfortunately brought me much emotional and physical pain, I decided to find something better. Not only for me during that time but for my future. A place that brought motivation to my dreams in the future. Which is why I now currently work in a high school, surrounded by teachers, councelors and students who also want a better and brighter future.

Now with my knowledge of how vital education is, I try to motivate the student and show them that it is possible to overcome these struggles no matter what.

What is this page?

This page is just a small view of what I have learned in my CS80 class while also showing a small dose of who I am.

I hope to use this page as an Academic Coach in Da Vinci Science. In hopes of creating a Web Development Seminar and showing some of you (the students) how to create this for yourselves. By the end of the seminar/course you will have a small project/portfolio that you could then present to your fellow classmates and teachers as a Presentation of Learning.

Although, this is still a very simple draft, I can see the potential this can have if I continue to work on it and present it to more students.
